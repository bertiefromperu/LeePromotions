About

An overview of what Lee Promotions is all about

  • Kenny Lee

    Lee Promotions is named after the late, great empresario, Kenny Lee who was renowned for his larger-than-life personality, entrepreneurial achievements and love for his city of birth, Cork. Kenny Lee always had strong passion for entertainment and his achievements highlight this! Pioneering the nightlife scene with the first plush nightclub in Cork City, Bogarts, followed by Spiders Nightclub and Bar. As well as working alongside Jim Aiken of Aiken Promotions, he was instrumental in the creation of Live at the Marquee Cork back in 2005. Lee Promotions aims to carry on the legacy of Kenny Lee through devoted passion to the entertainment industry of the city with the most vibrant and alive audiences in the world.

  • Our Story

    Cork has always been a vibrant epicentre of music, theatre and all walks of art. Lee Promotions was created in and for Cork, to act as a promotion and production service for local bands and theatre artists, aiming to provide them with a platform. Whether that be as a beginning block for emerging artists or, for some, as a boost in capital to their already established and honed artistry. Designed to scale their productions to the level their talent and craft deserves. We will also be a strong voice for the completion of the Cork Event Centre and all the opportunities it will bring. A saga which has been rumbling since 2007.

  • Our ambition

    Here at Lee Promotions we strive to promote live music and theatre that are happening around the city, while also prioritising all our clients and supporters' needs with our quality of service and hope of bringing the creativity and colourful arts of Cork City to life.
